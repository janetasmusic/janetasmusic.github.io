BIO

A powerful female vocal that California’s Rawckus Magazine compared to a mix of icons such as Avril Lavigne, Alanis Morissette and Lady Gaga. The Janet A project was born during the Covid era, when singer Janet’s original songs caught the attention of several music outlets and gradually evolved into a four-piece band. The group is built on the contrast between melody and guitar pressure, with a strong focus on atmosphere and a preference for its own mood and expression over clear genre labels. The band has released two EPs (2019 Janet A, 2021 Freak) and the recently released album From The Left To The Right End. Their journey has also included a performance at the Rock for People festival and work in Berlin’s Riverside Studios after winning a European competition hosted by Musichub.
